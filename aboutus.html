ABOUT

Asmibooks is a for-profit, socially conscious business and a global online bookseller that collects and sells new and used books online, matching each purchase with a book donation. Each sale generates funds for literacy and education initiatives in the U.S. and around the world. Since its launch in 2000, Asmibooks has raised $33 million for libraries and literacy, donated over 32 million books, and reused or recycled more than 397 million books.
